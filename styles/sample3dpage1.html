03 — Temporal Log

THE HISTORY
OF NOTHING

T–0
The Singularity Ignites
All of space, time, matter, and energy compressed into an infinitely dense point. Temperature exceeds all known scales. Physics breaks down.
T+380K
The First Light
Photons decouple from matter. The universe becomes transparent for the first time. Cosmic Microwave Background radiation fills all space.
T+1B
Galaxies Coalesce
Gravity pulls diffuse hydrogen into towering filaments. The first galaxies ignite. Quasars blaze at the centres of proto-galactic halos.
NOW
You Are Here
13.8 billion years later. Stardust assembled into a conscious observer, sitting at a screen, scrolling through the cosmos.
